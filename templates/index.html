Breast Cancer Prediction Form

This form uses data derived from the Breast Cancer Wisconsin Diagnostic Dataset. Each feature represents a measurement from a digitized image of a breast mass biopsy. After you submit the form, a machine learning model will predict whether the sample is likely benign or malignant.
